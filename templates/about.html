Our Methodology

Time Series Analysis

At the core of Stat Lemon's prediction engine is our advanced time series analysis framework, which uses ARIMA and SARIMA models to identify trends and patterns in player performance data.

Unlike conventional statistical approaches that look at season averages, our time series approach captures:

  • Performance trends over time
  • Seasonal patterns within seasons
  • Player-specific volatility
  • Response to different game contexts

Data Collection & Processing

We collect comprehensive player and game data from multiple reliable sources, including:

  • Official NBA API for game data and schedules
  • Advanced player statistics from multiple providers
  • Real-time lineup and injury information

Our data pipeline processes this information in real-time, ensuring that predictions are based on the most current data available.

Team Chemistry Modeling

One of Stat Lemon's unique features is our ability to model team chemistry and player compatibility. This component analyzes:

  • Historical performance of players together
  • Complementary skillsets
  • Playing style compatibility
  • Positional balance and coverage

This allows us to predict not just how individual players will perform, but how they'll perform together as a unit.
